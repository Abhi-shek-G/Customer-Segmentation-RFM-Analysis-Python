Champions:
1. Offer exclusive early access to new products.
2. Provide loyalty rewards and premium memberships.
3. Encourage referrals with incentives.

Loyal Customers:
1. Cross-sell and upsell related products.
2. Offer loyalty discounts.
3. Send personalized product recommendations.

Recent Customers:
1. Send welcome emails and onboarding offers.
2. Provide limited-time discount coupons.
3. Encourage second purchase with bundle deals.

At Risk:
1. Send re-engagement campaigns.
2. Offer special comeback discounts.
3. Conduct feedback surveys to understand churn reasons.

Others:
1. Run general marketing campaigns.
2. Promote seasonal discounts.
3. Educate about product benefits.
